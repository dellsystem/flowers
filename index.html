Flowers

a styling framework for people who like floral shirts and good design…

Pages

Many pages are built today in a scrolling-page layout, where the page is divided up into single sections, each taking up a proportion of the viewport.

Flowers  is designed with this in mind.

Flexible Boxes

Flowers  is built upon the flexible box module of HTML5. More known as flexbox, it is used for dynamic positioning on elements for both elements of known and unknown sizes.

Flowers  makes it easy to build responsive layouts.
